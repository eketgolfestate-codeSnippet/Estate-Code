Who We Are?

Our Vision

Our vision is to redefine luxury living in Akwa Ibom by providing a lifestyle where elegance meets tranquility. We are committed to creating a unique, sustainable community that caters to your comfort, safety, and well-being.



Our Story

Eket Golf Estate is a testament to our vision of creating luxurious living spaces where elegance meets comfort. This prestigious estate was brought to life by 7star Properties Ltd, the driving force behind the development of Eket Golf Estate.

Founded with a passion for redefining the real estate landscape in Akwa Ibom, 7star Properties Ltd has consistently delivered premium residential projects that blend modern design with high-quality amenities. Our journey began with a commitment to excellence, aiming to provide exclusive living experiences tailored to discerning homeowners.

As a company with a global outlook, 7star Properties Ltd is not just rooted in Nigeria but also extends its footprint internationally, with a branch office in the United States. This expansion allows us to bring world-class standards to every project we undertake.

At Eket Golf Estate, we are proud to offer an exceptional living environment that combines serene natural beauty with modern conveniences. Whether it’s the lush green golf course, 24-hour security, or state-of-the-art facilities, every feature has been carefully designed to enrich your lifestyle.

Join us at Eket Golf Estate, A Home In A Resort, brought to you by the trusted expertise of 7star Properties Ltd.

  • Exquisite Golf Course: Enjoy a world-class golfing experience with our well-maintained golf course, perfect for both beginners and seasoned players..
